Chapter 3: Advanced neural network architectures

This chapter will introduce both special techniques and architectures for learning embeddings (which are the backbone of basically every NLP algorithm) as well as specialized neural architectures. Specifically, we cover convolutional and recurrent networks, including special variants of the latter, and eventually introduce the encoder-decoder framework (also w/ attention), which is of great importance for the field.

    This chapter is about different (basic!) types of text tokenization used in NLP tasks.

  • Chapter 3.2: Word Embeddings

    This chapter is about the concept of word embeddings and the different methods for creating them.

  • Chapter 3.3: Convolutional Networks

    This chapter is about Convolutional Neural Networks and their applications.

  • Chapter 3.4: Recurrent Networks

    This chapter is about Recurrent Neural Networks and their applications.

  • Chapter 3.5: Attention

    This chapter is about attention mechanism and its application in neural networks.
